At transfer, the top fiber stress must not exceed tensile stress limit: \[ \sigma _{t} = -\frac{P _{i}}{A} + \frac{P _{i} \times e}{Z _{top}} - \frac{M _{o}}{Z _{top}} \leq F _{ti} \;\;\;(1) \] At transfer, the bottom fiber stress must not exceed compressive stress limit: \[ \sigma _{b} = -\frac{P _{i}}{A} - \frac{P _{i} \times e}{Z _{btm}} + \frac{M _{o}}{Z _{btm}} \geq F _{ci} \;\;\;(2) \] At transfer, the bottom fiber stress must not exceed tensile stress limit: \[ \sigma _{b} = -\frac{R \times P _{i}}{A} - \frac{R \times P _{i} \times e}{Z _{btm}} + \frac{M _{T}}{Z _{btm}} \leq F _{t} \;\;\;(3) \] At transfer, the top fiber stress must not exceed compressive stress limit: \[ \sigma _{t} = -\frac{R \times P _{i}}{A} + \frac{R \times P _{i} \times e}{Z _{top}} - \frac{M _{T}}{Z _{top}} \geq F _{c} \;\;\;(4) \] Note: in the 4 equations above, the first term is contribution of the axial force \( P _{i} \), the second term is contribution of the bending moment due to \( P _{i} \), the third term is the contribution of the bending moment due to either \( M _{o} \) or \( M _{t} \).
