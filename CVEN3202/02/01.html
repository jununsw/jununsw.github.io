Instruction

  • Drag the point to change the shape of the curve
  • By Ctrl + clicking the curve you can switch between the curves you want to edit (make sure your cursor overlaps the curve)
  • Use W, S, A, D to move the whole curren curve (since up and down key is occupied by scrolling)
