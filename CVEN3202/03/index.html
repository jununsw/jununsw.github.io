Instruction

  • A grain size distribution is shown below, to demonstrate soil classification
  • You can freely edit the shape of the soil
  • By holding CTRL key, five control points will be shown in the chart. Move these points horizontally to change the shape of the curve
  • You should move the red points first and then the purple points
  • Please note, in order to keep the curve realistic, range of the control points are limited subject to the shape of the curve
  • After finish editing the curve, press the 'Confirm' button below. You can re-edit the curve by pressing the 'Restart' button
