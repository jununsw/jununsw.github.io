A solid circular shaft with diameter of 36 mm is shown below. The ends A and D are fixed to the wall where rotating is prevented. Two Torques are applied on B and C. Given G = 80,000 MPa.

Please calculate the reaction Torque at A and D, as well as the angle of rotation at the centre of the shaft, E.

The torsional constant is calculated as:

J = (π/32) × 364 = 164.9 × 103 mm4.

The free-body diagram of the shaft is shown as:(with assumption of the directions of TA and TD)

Therefore we have the equation for equilibrium:    TA + TD = kN-m
And the equation for compatibility:    φAB + φBC + φCD = 0
That is: -TA × 2 + (-TA + 1.2) × 4 + TD × 4 = 0, So we can solve (rounded to 0.01):
TA = kN-m,
TD = kN-m,

Since φE = φAB + φBE
Thus we have φE = (-TA × lAB + (1.2 - TA) × lBE) / (G × J) = ° (Note: answer in degrees, round to 0.01)

Finally, please calculate the maximum shear stress τmax = Tmax • c / J: τmax = MPa (round to 0.01)
