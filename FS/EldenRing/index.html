Elden Ring DLC Walkthrough

    Gravesite Plain (Central)

    Scadutree Blessing: 1-3~ Revered Spirit Ash Blessing: 1-3~

    1. Activate Site of Grace
    2. Pick up Map: Gravesite Plain
    3. Fight Logur, the Beast Claw who drops the Beast Claw (Southeast of the first Site of Grace)
    4. Visit the Church of Consolation
    5. Go to Scorched Ruins and activate Site of Grace
    6. Get the Larval Tear at the very edge of Gravesite Plain, east of Scorched Ruins (nighttime only)
    7. Go to Western Nameless Mausoleum and defeat Blackgaol Knight
    8. Get the Sacred Tower Painting
    9. Defeat the Furnace Golem
    10. Go to the Three-Path Cross Site of Grace north of Scorched Ruins talk to Redmane Freyja and Hornsent
    11. Optional: Travel north to discover Abandoned Ailing Village and defeat the field boss: Ghostflame Dragon
    12. Take the Larval Tear south of Abandoned Ailing Village in an illuminated gravesite (nighttime only)
    13. Explore Belurat Gaol and Defeat the Side Boss: Demi-Human Swordmaster Onze

    Upon entering the Realm of Shadow, you will see a faint light glowing before you. Walk towards this and follow the steps leading to a view of the vast land you are now in. Activate the Gravesite Plain - Site of Grace ahead and interact with it so you have a checkpoint here. Walk northeast past the Site of Grace and look for the dirt path. Follow this path leading north and pick up the Map: Gravesite Plain in the stele to your right, like the one in the base game. This unlocks the Gravesite Plain on your map and will help you navigate more easily.

    While in this area, be on the lookout for a Curseblade, this is an enemy with two blades that will attack you. After defeating it, head south this time and follow the dirt path. You can summon Torrent here so you can cover more distance. You can also pick up items along the way to help with crafting like Redflesh Mushroom, Whiteflesh Mushroom, and Rada Fruit on the ground. You will also encounter shadow enemies here that can attack you while riding your mount. Stay on this dirt path and continue up until you see the thick woods to the south.

    Stray from the dirt path for a bit and go into the woods. Listen and follow the bird sounds while in this area. You will come across a hostile NPC called Logur the Beast Claw in the middle of the woods. This NPC drops the Beast Claw (Weapon) upon defeating it.

    Return to the dirt path and continue until you see a broken cart and dead bodies. Take a moment here to pick up the following items: Glass Shard, Black Pyrefly, and x10 Arrow. Then, continue following the dirt path until you reach the area outside the Church of Consolation. You can pick up Dewgem on top of the stones surrounding the church and in the area close to the nearby waterfall. You can follow the cliff wall going north, in which you will find a corpse containing a Blessing of Marika surrounded by Messmer Soldiers. Go back to the church, and you will find a Black Pyrefly behind it.

    Inside the church, you will find a Black Knight patrolling the aisle. It's up to you if you want to kill it or you can go around the church, stay crouched, and sneak in front of the altar to pick up x2 Scadutree Fragment. However, if you opt to defeat the enemy, you can obtain Black Steel Greathammer, Black Knight Helm, and Smithing Stone [6] as a reward. Then, take the Scadutree Fragment in front of the altar. You can use this to level up your Scadutree Blessing while resting on a Site of Grace. Outside the church, there's a small camp of Messmer Soldiers to the east. You can eliminate these soldiers for a chance to loot a piece of their armor and then go inside the round tent to pick up x2 Fire Coil from a corpse. This is your last stop in the southern area.

    Return to the dirt path and head north this time. Stay close to the right side where you can pick up a Larval Tear at the edge of the cliff here. [See Shadow of the Erdtree Map]. This item can only be picked up at night and can be found in the center of a small illuminated gravesite.

    When you're done here, scale the edge and proceed to the northeastern cliff where you see the waterfall. Go to the small altar and pick up the Revered Spirit Ash, this is also a new item that lets you boost Spirit Ashes beyond +10 and increase the durability and damage dealt by Torrent within the realm of shadow. From the small altar, continue exploring Gravesite Plain by sticking to the edge of the map. You will come across a small gravesite area with Shadow Undead enemies. Make sure to eliminate the glimmering Shadowpot to get the Scadutree Fragment drop [See it on the SOTE Map] and other crafting materials. You can also pick up a Spiritgrave Stone from one of the stone heads and x3 Black Pyrefly on the ground.

    After that, head west to reach Scorched Ruins. This is a small area where you can fight some enemies and pick up important items and the first thing you will do is to activate the Site of Grace found north of the building. If you have been following this Walkthrough, take the time to use the Revered Spirit Ash and Scadutree Fragment you collected while resting on the Site of Grace.

    From the Scorched Ruins - Site of Grace, walk beneath the building and go over to the southern side. Turn around and climb the stairs to your left, and take the Hefty Cracked Pot from the corpse. This is a material that can be used for crafting cracked pot items. You can also loot other crafting items on the ground here. Continue to your right and take the stairs to the destroyed room. Then, pick up the Greater Potentate's Cookbook [1] from a corpse on the stairs here. Next, go inside the broken building and look for the corpse hidden within jars by destroying it. You can pick up the Shadow Realm Rune here. Go to the second floor and walk towards the enemies attacking you from the opposite building. Cross the roof to the other side and pick up Black Pyrefly behind this room. Then, go back to the front of this room and open the door to reveal a chest containing the Blade of Mercy (Talisman). After that, drop down to the balcony below it. Jump to the next platform and pick up the Bone Dart from a corpse. This is the quick coverage of Scorched Ruins.

    The next location is an optional dungeon where you can fight a boss for a reward. Head west where you see white withered trees in the distance. As you get near a tall gravestone, 2 Gravebirds will swoop down to attack you. Continue traveling west and go past the glowing Blue Worm creatures. Continue riding Torrent until you reach the Western Nameless Mausoleum. You can go inside this building to defeat the Blackgaol Knight and earn the Greatsword of Solitude and Solitude Set as a reward.

    After fighting the Blackgaol Knight, return to Scorched Ruins - Site of Grace and then continue exploring the northeastern side by scaling the edge of the plain. Keep an eye out for a cave here with a glowing light. Go inside the cave and interact with The Sacred Tower Painting. [See the location on Elden Ring Shadow of the Erdtree Map]. As you head back outside, you will come across Inquisitor enemies who use Fire and Magic. Go toward the small altar with 4 pillars located behind this pack of enemies to pick up the Backhand Blade. Continue along the cliff and take the Grave Glovewort from the corpse on the stone. You might come across a Gravebird here again.

    Back to the dirt path along Scorched Ruins, you will encounter the Furnace Golem, an elite enemy that drops runes, Deflecting Hardtear, and Furnace Visage upon death. You will also see more of this enemy in other locations, but each of them has a unique tear drop. This enemy will also start throwing projectiles at you if you get too close.

    • The Furnace Golem can be tough to beat because of the wide AoE attacks that it can do. It will take some time to get used to but the movement is slow and can be dodged by timing it with a jump before its foot hits the ground. If you're having a hard time, you can ride Torrent while fighting this boss and then focus on attacking the legs. You can take advantage of Torrent's double jump so you can have more air time.

    Proceed north by tracing the dirt path and stop where you see a trace of light glowing from the ground, and some figures are gathered near it. Head towards them and make sure to activate the Three-Path Cross Site of Grace here. Talk to the NPCs, who you will come to know as followers of Miquella. Just like Needle Knight Leda said, these followers will offer help in your journey in tracing Miquella's footsteps. You can pick up an Empyrean-Blood Burgeon and a Scadutree Fragment on the ground next to the cross. Interact with the NPCs here and make sure to exhaust the dialogues of Hornsent to receive the Cross Map from him. The other NPC is Redmane Freyja who you can speak with to get more info about Hornsent. Exhaust her dialogues before you continue exploring the northernmost area.

    From the Three-Path Cross Site of Grace, head to the area below the greatbridge. Look for a path on the left side and carefully jump down. Stick to the wall and take the Ash of War: Savage Lion's Claw from a small camp here. Then, face the east direction toward the bridge to find a way to the lower area here. While going down, at night, you can see the blue glow of a Larval Tear. You can also go to the Run-Down Traveler's Rest, which is directly below the bridge and you can pick up the Greater Potentate's Cookbook [2] inside. When you're done here, you can travel north to reach your next destination, which is the Abandoned Ailing Village.

    To reach Abandoned Ailing Village, stick to the edge of the cliff here and travel north. Continue until you reach a dead-end where you will find a small altar with the Revered Spirit Ash. Eliminate the nearby giant moths so you can safely take this item. Then, circle back and go to the ramp so you can activate the Greatbridge, North - Site of Grace.

    You will see a ghost facedown on the ground ahead of you. Interact with it before you proceed to Abandoned Ailing Village. Go to the edge of the cliff behind the ghost and pick up the Human Bone Shard from a corpse. Continue walking at the edge and you will find another corpse on the wooden platform where you can obtain the Ailment Talisman. Before leaving this area, go to the other side of the houses (across the ghost) and you will find a Broken Rune from a corpse.

    You can now travel west toward the lake and you will see a Ghostflame Dragon in the distance. There's a summoning pool that you can activate if you want to use it. Defeating this boss will reward you with x1 Dragon Heart and x1 Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone. See the Ghostflame Dragon for information and strategies to defeating this boss. You can also pick up a Great Katana from a corpse next to the dragon. After this fight, continue exploring north where you will find a small shack below the Abandoned Ailing Village. There's a corpse outside at roof level where you can pick up Fly Mold, then, you can jump on top of the house and drop down through the hole in the roof to pick up the Greater Potentate's Cookbook [10].

    Next, head west into an area in Gravebird statues. You can pick up the Gravebird Armor from the altar in the middle here. Then, follow the body of water leading to metal barricades scattered on the ground. This will lead to an entrance to Belurat Gaol where you can defeat an optional boss called Demi-Human Swordmaster Onze to earn Demi-Human Swordsman Yosh (Spirit Ash) as a reward.

    The Belurat Gaol is an optional dungeon situated under the Belurat Tower Settlement. The entrance can be accessed by heading west from the Abandoned Ailing Village or the Greatbridge, North Site of Grace. You will follow the body of water here and you'll know that you are at the right location when you reach the part where you see metal barricades and prison cages. As you enter the dungeon, you will find the Belurat Gaol Site of Grace and a Summoning Pool on the right side of the entrance.

    This area has enemies such as Revered Spirits, large and small variations of the Living Jar, and Mutated Prisoners. This area is also a good place to farm or find Frozen Maggots. The objective is to reach the bottom-most section of the gaol where a mini-boss is waiting.

    As you formally set foot in the location, head down the steps on the left and be ready to block or dodge, a Shadow Undead will ambush you as soon as you approach a doorway along the steps. Head inside the room on the left and you'll find a body you can loot to find x6 Frozen Maggot. Go back outside, and continue going down the steps until you reach the end where the ground collapses. Kill more of the Revered Spirits, and if you look to your left, you'll find another doorway. Be ready again for another spirit who will try to ambush you, kill the enemies, and loot the body lying on the ground to find x1 Silver Horn Tender.

    Go back outside and cross the narrow path to reach the other end. But tread carefully since enemies are roaming around and blocking your path. Kill the two spirits and before you go through the doorway ahead, there is another body you can loot by the edge of the cliff. Loot it to find x4 Frozen Maggot. Go through the doorway and look to your right to see a hole that you can jump through. Proceed to this path, and then jump through the hole on the left side to continue your descent.

    Enter the room that looks like a storeroom that is filled with crates, cages, and tables. Kill the enemies and loot the body next to the shelf with barrels on the left side to find x1 Broken Rune. Exit the room, but before you enter the next room on the left, break the crates on the right to find another corpse lying by the edge of a cliff to find x1 Thawfrost Boluses. As you pass through the connecting room, you'll see a Ghost Prisoner NPC that you can speak to who begs for forgiveness and fears the jar.

    Upon exiting, you'll come across a section of the prison where you'll see large jars chained together and onto the ceiling. Before you make your descent by using the jars as platforms, look ahead to find another doorway that leads to a room with jars and crates. There is a body here that you can loot to find x1 Shadow Realm Rune [3], but at the same time, you'll fight against a Mutated Prisoner, humanoids that have mutated and erupted from their jars. Once you've cleared the room, return to the hanging jars to start descending to the bottom area (there's a body on top of the hanging jar that has x1 Glass Shard). As indicated in the image above, you'll want to try and make your way down towards the side of the room that has a wooden platform. There is a chest there that contains x1 Hefty Cracked Pot.

    After getting the item, continue to the bottom and proceed west. Clear the enemies along the way and pass through the doorway where you'll enter a shrine-looking section of the gaol. Drop onto the bridge and fight your way against more enemies. When you reach the opposite end, as you approach the altar, the ground beneath you will collapse and you'll fall into a mini-labyrinth that's filled with small and large jars. Take caution here as both Living Jar and Mutated Prisoners are hiding in corners, waiting to ambush you.

    Take the time here to explore the section with pots since there are some items that you can loot: x1 Lost Ashes of War, x2 Smithing Stone [6], x1 Greater Potentate's Cookbook (11). There are also x1 Hefty Cracked Pot, and x1 Innard Meat looted off the bodies that are on top of the large jars. Once you're done clearing the room, proceed east to find the exit leading to the next section. Kill the Living Jar and loot the body sitting on a prison cage to find x2 Smithing Stone [3].

    Before advancing the dungeon further, head west on your compass, and you will find a jar that is slightly at a lower level than your position. Jump on it and it will take you up high. Once it stops, jump towards the following platforms and continue forward. You will find Greatjar on a pedestal. Move forward and drop down to find yourself again in the Jar Labyrinth. Resume your previous way, to continue advancing in the dungeon.

    Next, jump across to reach the opposite side of the area to find a golden mist door, and fight the Mutated Prisoners. Position your compass towards the South, and you'll see a chained jar that you can jump on. Use it as a platform to jump across a broken pillar, and continue jumping down until you reach the end, where you'll find a gold mist door. Before you enter, make preparations, as you'll fight against the Demi-Human Swordmaster Onze upon passing through the mist.

    The Demi-Human Swordmaster Onze boss is an agile boss capable of leaping into the air, using lunge attacks, and executing deadly combos similar to a samurai. In our playthrough, our character's build focuses on melee and we had a shield equipped to mitigate attacks as much as possible. As soon as you enter prepare to dodge or block as the boss will teleport to you and follow up with a swinging attack. Since we were facing the boss without the help of other players, we decided to summon the Black Knife Tiche to keep things in check.

    The Black Knife Tiche in our opinion is good against this boss since it also moves fast and is capable of attacking its target in close or mid-range. As soon as the boss switched its attention towards our summoned spirit, we took the opportunity to land some attacks before it switched its attention to the character. Play it safe and keep an eye out for its movements since the boss moves quickly. Defeating it rewards the spirit ash Demi-Human Swordsman Yosh.

    To return to the entrance, use the pool of energy at the center of the boss arena to teleport to the entrance of this dungeon, or simply return to the Three-Path Cross - Site of Grace to continue your progression.

    Gravesite Plain (Western)

    Scadutree Blessing: 1-3~ Revered Spirit Ash Blessing: 1-3~

    1. From the Three-Path Cross - Site of Grace: Reach Cliffroad Terminus Site of Grace
    2. Explore Prospect Town
    3. Head to the gravesite to get Larval Tear and Scadutree Fragment
    4. Look for the Forager Brood who will give you the Forager Brood Cookbook (2). This NPC can be found next to the illuminated gravesite at night, which surrounds a Larval Tear at night. NOTE: Harming the Forager Brood will cause Moore to appear and invade your world. If Moore dies, this will also cause the Forager Brood to disappear permanently.
    5. Get the Incursion Painting in Artist's Shack
    6. Explore the Church of Benediction

    After exploring Central Gravesite Plain, you will go to the Western section. You can access this area by passing through the archway that Redmane Freyja is facing, but instead of going up the stairs, head towards the left and stick to the mountainside on your left. You will come across shadow enemies here that you can eliminate for a chance to get Rada Fruit from them. Go to the left after exiting the archway and navigate the ledge here. You will face hounds and giant bats along the way but you can safely outrun them if you're riding Torrent.

    Stick to the wall on your left and turn left as you see the opening. After scaling the cliff here, the first thing you will be doing is to activate the Cliffroad Terminus and the path here can be split into three:

    • First: Explore the gravesite to southwest. This is where you will look for the glimmering Shadowpot.
    • Second: Explore Prospect Town directly south of the grace; there's a chest at the top of this building that contains the Outer God Heirloom and a number of items that you can pick up from the ground. Past this area, you will find the Forager Brood and the Larval Tear at night at the center of a small illuminated gravesite.
    • Third: Go past the lake to the east and explore the southern area towards the Artist's Shack and Church of Benediction. You will pick up the Incursion Painting from the shack. Once you reach the church, there's an area on the cliffside where you can loot Pata (a weapon) and the Oathseeker Knight Set.

    From here, follow the dirt path and head southwest. This will lead you to a gravesite with more Shadow enemies. Go near the edge and look for an item on the ground near the gravestone heads to obtain the following items:

    • Scadutree Fragment
    • Silver Horn Tender
    • Rada Fruit

    There's also a Grave Glovewort that you can collect from one of the corpses here. After this area, head to the opposite side of the cliff and go toward the pond. You will encounter Hounds and Bloodfiend enemies here along the way.

    At the edge of the pond, you will see a small altar where you can pick up a Revered Spirit Ash. Then, head south of the pond where a hound and Bloodfiends are gathered. You can pick up Great Arrow and a Smithing Stone from their gathering spot. Continue exploring until you reach the edge south of the pond. You will find a non-hostile servant here who will give you the Forager Brood Cookbook [2] when interacted with. There's also a nearby illuminated grave where you can pick up a Larval Tear from the center. (This area can only be spotted at night.)

    Continue navigating this area and go toward the cave where you will encounter a Giant Rat and another Curseblade. You're heading toward where you interacted with the first Site of Grace in the Shadow of Realm but in the area above it. As you reach the edge of the cliff, you will see the "Incursion" Painting inside the Artist's Shack. [See the location on Elden Ring Shadow of the Erdtree Map] After going here, explore the southern area to reach the Church of Benediction. Go toward the center and pick up the Blessed Blue Dew Talisman from the altar. Then, face north and exit the tall and slim archway toward the cliff. Pick up Pata and the Oathseeker Knight Set here.

    You can go down here and scale the wall while riding Torrent. This gives you access back to the Gravesite Plain without going back to the Site of Grace in Crossroad Terminus.

    Belurat Tower Settlement

    Scadutree Blessing: 4~ Revered Spirit Ash Blessing: 4~

    1. Activate the Site of Grace and the Summoning Pool
    2. Meet Moore and Sir Ansbach
    3. Defeat the NPC Invader Fire Knight Queelign.
    4. Take the Storeroom Key and unlock the door near the Small Private Altar Site of Grace to meet Hornsent Grandam
    5. Take the Well Depths Key and head to the Sewers
    6. Navigate the Stagefront section of Belurat Tower Settlement
    7. Defeat the Divine Beast Dancing Lion
    8. After defeating the boss, pick up the Tower of Shadow Message
    9. Equip the Divine Beast Head armor piece and return to Hornsent Grandam to receive Watchful Spirit (Incantation)
    10. After exploring Belurat Tower Settlement, you must speak with Ansbach at the Three-Path Cross Site of Grace to progress his questline

    Go back to the archway, but now follow the stairs. When you arrive at the main gate of Belurat Tower Settlement, use the Main Gate Cross - Site of Grace, and speak with 2 NPCs near it. You will come to know them as Moore and Sir Ansbach. Speak to Sir Ansbach and exhaust his dialogue where he talks to you about the crosses that are akin to Miquella's footprints. He will ask you to find them and speak to him upon discovering the other crosses. Behind Sir Ansback is Moore, a merchant you can interact with to buy items. Next to Miquella's Cross, where Sir Ansbach is standing, you can pick up x1 Scadutree Fragment on the ground.

    Once you are done speaking to the NPCs or resting at the site of grace, climb to the top of the tall stairs and open the double doors to enter the legacy dungeon. As you approach the first set of stairs, a large Spider Scorpion ahead will drop from the ceiling and start to attack you. Be ready to dodge as it will jump in the air. The large creature is easy to kill since you can block its attacks with your shield or dodge it at the right time. After killing it, climb to the top and enter the large room.

    Head up to the second floor and you'll fight against another large Spider Scorpion, kill it, and near it, you can find another body on the ground. Loot it to find x1 Fireproof Dried Liver. Proceed to the next section and head up the stairs where you'll encounter another large Spider Scorpion. As you exit, you'll find another body by the stairs, loot it to find x5 Black Pyrefly. Exit through the door at the top of the steps to formally arrive at the Belurat Tower Settlement, you can find the Belurat Tower Settlement Site of Grace and a Summoning Pool at the top of the stone steps, near the double doors, on the left side.

    Inside, you'll find a statue in the middle of the room. Tread carefully as you approach it since there are multiple Spider Scorpion enemies around the statue. Killing the enemies will have a chance to drop Scorpion Liver. Approach the statue after clearing the room to find x2 Revered Spirit Ash. Behind the statue, on the right side, there is a body sitting by the wall, you can pillage its remains to find x1 Shadow Realm Rune [1].

    Head up to the second floor and you'll fight against another large Spider Scorpion, kill it, and near it, you can find another body on the ground. Loot it to find x1 Fireproof Dried Liver. Proceed to the next section and head up the stairs where you'll encounter another large Spider Scorpion. As you exit, you'll find another body by the stairs, loot it to find x5 Black Pyrefly. Exit through the door at the top of the steps to formally arrive at the Belurat Tower Settlement, you can find the Belurat Tower Settlement Site of Grace and a Summoning Pool at the top of the stone steps, near the double doors, on the left side.

    Next to the site of grace, there is a room you can enter (Ignore the large stone door since this is locked). Defeat the two Revered Spirit enemies and on the right side of the locked door, there is a body on the ground you can loot to find x1 Neutralizing Boluses. Go back outside, and head east towards an open section where you'll find a fountain and a waterfall. Around the fountain, there are hostile spirits that are praying to the statue sitting on the fountain. Kill them and you'll also find another body that you can loot to acquire x1 Stimulating Boluses. On the left side, you'll see a stream of water coming from above, approach it to find another body that can be looted to find x3 Knot Resin.

    On the right-hand side of the fountain, there is a short set of stairs that goes up. Head towards that direction and then make a right where you'll find another fountain with a group of spirits praying. You can kill the enemies easily and find x1 Somber Smithing Stone [3] on the ground. By the fountain where you got the smithing tone, if you look to the left, you'll see a ladder that goes down to the sewer or well depths. The door there is locked and you need to find the Well Depths Key to unlock it. However, you can go ahead and check the area to find x1 Messmerfire Grease on the pile of dead bodies. Climb back up the ladder and explore the east section of the area.

    As you walk towards the path, be ready to dodge the magical attacks from the enemies positioned above. Be ready to dash towards the slope that goes up where you'll encounter more Revered Spirits. As you run forward, go right to find an alleyway where you'll find a body. Loot it to find x1 Messmerfire Grease. Go back to the main path and head up the stairs. There are about three of these enemies that wield a melee weapon. Kill the enemies and you loot the nearby corpse to find x3 Black Pyrefly. Turn your camera facing north and you'll find an entrance to a kitchen room. There are three spirit enemies inside the kitchen room. Kill the one standing near the vases and break the objects to find x1 Silver Horn Tender on the ground.

    Head up the stairs and be ready to dodge the mage spirit from its magical attack. Kill it and just next to the arched doorway is the Small Private Altar site of grace. Go through the doorway after activating the site of grace and you'll find a Summoning Pool by the wall. You can explore the rooftops area through the doorway that faces the Summoning Pool to find some items there but tread carefully as there are enemies around.

    On the rooftops, there's a body tucked by a corner that holds x1 Somber Smithing Stone [4]. To find it, go straight until you reach the end towards the locked door of the storeroom, jump over the broken railings on the right, then immediately turn right again. At the end, drop onto the roof, walk straight, and jump onto the narrow beam on the left to reach the rooftop across. Still, on the rooftop section, you can find a body hanging by the edge of one of the roofs. Loot it to find x1 Silver Horn Tender.

    Return to the Small Private Altar site of grace and proceed to the doorway on the right. At the top of the stairs, be ready to fight against an elite enemy, a dual sword-wielding Horned Warrior. After killing the enemy, proceed east towards the rubble of destroyed buildings where you'll encounter more Revered Spirits, there's a body you can loot to find x2 Black Pyrefly, and another body by a corner, loot it to find x4 Thin Beast Bones. Continue going straight until you enter a large section that has an altar and a tree in the center. As you approach the altar, an NPC invader, Fire Knight Queelign (NPC), will appear and fight you. This NPC invader uses fire-based attacks. (Assuming that this is your first encounter against Fire Knight Queelign), successfully killing Fire Knight Queelign, drops the Crusade Insignia talisman.

    • NOTE: If this is your first time encountering the NPC Invader, Fire Knight Queelign, defeating him drops the Crusade Insignia talisman. However, if it's your second encounter, he drops the Prayer Room Key and Ash of War: Flame Skewer. For added information, the other location where Fire Knight Queelign appears as an NPC Invader is at the Church of the Crusade.

    Investigate the altar to find a body that you can pillage to find the Ash of War: Shriek of Sorrow, as well as other bodies around the area to find items such as x10 Arrow looted off a body that's hanging by the edge of some rubble, x3 Black Pyrefly from a body sitting on a bench. Return to the entrance of the altar and navigate to the upper area of the rubble. Proceed north of your compass where you'll find an area where a stream of water is flowing, there's a body next to the steel grate that you can loot to obtain x5 Redflesh Mushroom, and x4 String you can loot by the edge of the stream of water.

    Proceed through the doorway on the right to find a storeroom. Climb up the ladder and you'll see a body sitting against a wall next to another arched doorway. Loot it to find the Storeroom Key. The Storeroom Key can be used to unlock the door near the Small Private Altar Site of Grace. Inside that room, you can find an NPC Hornsent Grandam.

    Exit through the doorway and turn left. By the steps, jump on the narrow side of the tiled roof and walk around it. But before you do that, go down the stairs until you reach a dead-end, there's a body hanging by the edge that you can loot to find x3 Throwing Dagger. Now, (refer to the image above) jump across the passageway and enter the room facing east. You should also find a body sitting against the railings, loot it to find x6 Whiteflesh Mushrooms. Enter the room ahead where you'll fight another spirit enemy and loot the body in the room to find the Well Depths Key. This key can be used to open the locked door down at the sewers of the well.

    The area ahead is polluted with poison, so be ready to have Neutralizing Boluses consumable items to alleviate the build-up of poison or to cure yourself from the effect. This section of the dungeon is also swarming with another type of enemy, the Man-Fly. They can easily be killed but are deadly if they attack you in a group. To find and unlock a shortcut to the Belurat Tower Settlement site of grace, look southeast of the compass to find a tower with a tall ladder. At the top, you can unlock the door to open the shortcut leading to the Belurat Tower Settlement Site of Grace.

    An elite enemy, a toxic variant of the Lesser Ulcerated Tree Spirit, can also be found south of the poison-filled area. You must kill the elite enemy to continue further, and eliminating it drops the Immunizing Horn Charm +2.     Game Progress Route Quick Summary

    There are 5 Map Fragments for Realm of Shadow:

    • Map: Gravesite Plain
    • Map: Scadu Altus
    • Map: Rauh Ruins
    • Map: Southern Shore
    • Map: Abyss

    NOTE: This is a summarized version of the full Game Progress Route which covers only the important story-related events. This gives you a quick read of the game's progress from start to finish, objectives per region, important locations, and spares exploration details.

    Gravesite Plain

    • Meet all NPCs: Redmane Freyja, Hornsent, Moore, Sir Ansbach, Hornsent Grandam
    • Go to Belurat Tower Settlement to progress Hornsent Grandam and Redmane Freyja's questlines
    • Encounter Fire Knight Queelign inside Belurat Tower Settlement
    • Cross the bridge to Eastern Gravesite Plain and meet Igon and Thiollier

    After arriving at the Realm of Shadow, go up the stairs to formally enter Gravesite Plain and then, use the Site of Grace. Remember to take the map fragment every time you arrive at a new region to help you navigate easily. Head north and go past the building, this is Scorched Ruins. You can stop by here to get crafting materials. Head over to the Three-Path Cross Site of Grace and speak to the NPCs. You will find Redmane Freyja and Hornsent there. Make sure to exhaust their dialogues to receive the Cross Map from Hornsent, this is a key item that will map out Miquella's Cross locations where he abandons a piece of his godhood. Note that you can find important items like the Empyrean-Blood Burgeon and Scadutree Fragment at these locations. This doesn't affect your story progression, but will boost your stats while in Shadow Realm.

    From here, go northwest to enter Belurat Tower Settlement. Outside this dungeon, you will also meet Sir Ansbach and Moore. Sir Ansbach was once a follower of Mohg, while Moore is a merchant in the Shadow Realm. Make sure to buy the Note: Sealed Spiritsprings from him and exhaust his dialogues. For Sir Ansbach, he will ask you to locate Miquella's Crosses during your conversation with him. Proceed to the dungeon where you will fight the Divine Beast Dancing Lion. You can summon Redmane Freyja for this fight but isn't vital to her story progression.

    NOTE: We recommend looking for Fire Knight Queelign (NPC) and let him invade you here so you can get an additional reward once you meet him again at the Church of the Crusade in Scadu Altus.

    After defeating the dungeon's boss, speak with Hornsent Grandam while wearing the Divine Beast Head to receive Watchful Spirit Incantation. Then, return to the Three-Path Cross Site of Grace, talk to the Redmane Freyja about her armor, and then cross the Greatbridge to the eastern side. You're right outside the gates of Castle Ensis but before you go in, travel to Pillar Path Waypoint to meet Igon and Thiollier. It is also recommended to reach the Dragon's Pit Site of Grace so you can come back here later when needed.

    Castle Ensis

    • Fight Rellana to reach Scadu Altus
    • NOTE: You can also reach Scadu Altus through a sealed spiritspring behind Fort of Reprimand if you want to skip Castle Ensis entirely. However, we recommend the Castle Ensis progression as this is the most straightforward path and more rewarding than skipping this dungeon.
    • Take the loot behind Castle Ensis Moongazing Grounds through an archway

    When you're ready, explore Castle Ensis and eliminate Rellana, Twin Moon Knight. Defeating this boss will open the gate to the next region, Scadu Altus. Before leaving the castle, through the archway on the northern side of the room. Follow the path but traverse it near the cliffside on the left. Drop down the cliff, then follow the stream going back to Castle Ensis. You will eventually arrive at a rampart in Castle Ensis in which you will find a chest. Open the chest, and you will receive the Carian Sorcery Sword.

    Scadu Altus

    • Reunite with Needle Knight Leda and Hornsent at the Highroad Cross - Site of Grace
    • Travel to the Church of the Crusade and fight Fire Knight Queelign there to obtain the Prayer Room Key (you will use this later at Shadow Keep)
    • Head to Moorth Ruins and explore the tunnel connecting the location to Bonny Village
    • Take the O Mother Gesture in an area near the Bonny Village
    • Cross the eastern bridge to Scadu Altus plain
    • Go to the Cathedral of Manus Metyr to speak with Count Ymir and Jolan (This interaction will initiate their questlines)

    Exit through the newly opened gate after defeating Rellana in Castle Ensis. Travel north by following the main path and you will come across Needle Knight Leda and Hornsent at the Highroad Cross - Site of Grace. Make sure to pick up the Monk's Missive and the gesture in this location. After this meeting, head north to pick up the Map: Scadu Altus, then head west to the Church of the Crusade and fight Fire Knight Queelign (NPC) there. If you went to Belurat Tower Settlement and fought him there, you could pick up an Ash of War: Flame Skewer from him. This only drops during the second encounter, and you can still get it later on at Shadow Keep.

    After this, head south and explore Moorth Ruins which contains a tunnel access to Bonny Village. You could also head north from Moorth Ruins to reach Rauh Base, but since there are no NPC interactions there, we can skip the entire location. For now, you will explore the area underneath Moorth Ruins and locate the tunnel connecting it to Bonny Village.

    Once you reach the Site of Grace in Bonny Village, use it and look for the O Mother (Gesture) [See it on the map here]. This is an important item that you will need later on to progress questlines. To continue your exploration, cross the bridge to the eastern side and head to the Cathedral of Manus Metyr.

    As you head north following the path here, you will receive a notification saying that a great rune has been broken, and a charm as well (This event opens up the path to St. Trina and you can now head to Cerulean Coast in Southern Shore to progress her questline after Scadu Altus. This event also marks changes with NPCs and it is recommended to travel to their respective Site of Graces to talk to them.

    Continue traveling north until you reach the Cathedral of Manus Metyr, go inside and speak with Count Ymir and Jolan. Exhaust all their dialogues to initiate their quests. When you're done, you will now head to Shadow Keep - Church District. The reason that we're skipping Rauh Base in the summarized version is because there are no NPC interactions there, and is a location that doesn't play a role in NPC progression. You can still explore Rauh Base if you wish before entering Shadow Keep.

    Shadow Keep - Church District

    • NOTE: Speak with all NPCs before entering Shadow Keep, especially after you get the loud notification that a great rune has been broken.
    • The Church District is a location that's inaccessible from the Main Gate because this area is flooded and you need to drain the water first.
    • Use the Prayer Room Key (from Church of the Crusade) and speak with Fire Knight Queelign (decide if you want to give Iris of Grace or Iris of Occultation)
    • Defeat the Scadutree Avatar
    • Find a way to reach Shadow Keep, Back Gate - Site of Grace (area before Commander Gaius fight and the entrance point to Hinterlands in Scaduview)

    Your goal in this location is to fully explore the back section of Shadow Keep, drain the water to access the lower area, and an optional fight against the Scadutree Avatar. You can skip it too if you like, but you can earn good rewards from defeating it, like its remembrance. The important NPC interaction here is to find Fire Knight Queelign's room and use the Prayer Room Key that you obtained at the Church of the Crusade and use it here. Inside this room, you will decide if you want to get his greatsword or his spirit ashes which you will do by giving him either an Iris of Grace or Iris of Occultation.

    After this interaction, you need to look for the way to Shadow Keep, Back Gate, and use the site of grace so you can come back here later to explore Scaduview and Hinterlands. For now, you will return to Castle Front - Site of Grace (outside Castle Ensis to progress Thiollier and St. Trina's questlines). You will do this by navigating the path to St. Trina, and at this point, you have broken Miquella's Charm and revealed the access to Stone Coffin Fissure where you will meet St. Trina later on.

    Cerulean Coast (Southern Shore)

    • Teleport to Castle Front - Site of Grace
    • Follow the river to Southern Shore
    • Explore Stone Coffin Fissure
    • Reach the Garden of Deep Purple
    • Speak with St. Trina
    • Progress Thiollier's questline (teleport to Pillar Path Waypoint)

    Teleport to the Site of Grace outside Castle Ensis and follow the path leading to the Ellac River (under the bridge) to make your way to Southern Shore. You can refer to the guide above to see the full details of how to get there. You will know that you're on Cerulean Coast when you see the fields full of blue flowers. Your main objective here is to reach the bottom of Stone Coffin Fissure and defeat the Putrescent Knight. This encounter will reveal the entrance to St. Trina's location and will also initiate her questline related to Miquella's story.

    After speaking with St. Trina and exhausting all of her dialogues, make sure to speak with Moore first, then return to Thiollier. You have an opportunity to receive the Black Syrup from Moore if you tell him about Thiollier. Then, bring this item to Thiollier and he will give you Thiollier's Concoction (you can use this item for an alternate ending for Dragon Communion Priestess questline if you want to receive the Dragonbolt of Florissax and Ancient Dragon Florissax (more on that if you visit her page).

    To progress Thiollier's questline, return to his location at the Pillar Path Cross - Site of Grace and let him know of your discovery about St. Trina's location. This will prompt him to move if you rest at a grace, so if you're trying to get the incantation and the spirit ashes from the dragon priestess, make sure to exchange the Thiollier's Concoction now. You will find him next at the Garden of Deep Purple, teleport there using the site of grace, and exhaust his dialogues. Imbibe the nectar 4 times. On the 4th one, you will begin to hear St. Trina's voice, and you can pass on St. Trina's words to Thiollier. Doing this will anger him and will prompt him to attack you in your world. Defeat Thiollier to receive St. Trina's Smile and after the fight, exhaust his dialogues again. Leave him be for now, and imbibe the nectar again before leaving this area.

    Continuing your story progress, head back up to Cerulean Coast and make your way to Finger Ruins of Rhia. Your goal here is to progress the questline of Count Ymir and Jolan, and you need to reach the finger and sound the bell using the item Count Ymir gave you and you can also refer to the map given by him as well.

    After this area, head to the Cathedral of Manus Metyr to speak with Count Ymir again. Report to him about your discovery at the Finger Ruins of Rhia and you will receive the second map from him.

    Charo's Hidden Grave (Entrance from Dragon's Pit)

    Now that you have progressed the questline of Count Ymir and Jolan and Thiollier and St. Trina, you will head next to Grand Altar of Dragon Communion (near Charo's Hidden Grave) to meet another NPC. This is the area above Cerulean Coast, where the field is filled with red flowers. You can reach this area by exploring Dragon's Pit in Scadu Altus. At the very end of this dungeon, you will fight the Ancient Dragon-Man to reveal the path to the dragon area.

    This will also let you access various areas like Charo's Hidden Grave, the Grand Altar of Dragon Communion, and the Jagged Peak. Make sure to meet the Dragon Communion Priestess and accept the Ancient Dragon's Blessing offer from her. The rest of the area here is optional, but will give you great rewards. This boss is optional but the area behind him will provide items to boost your stats. Then, return to the Site of Grace and enter the other room where you see an altar in the middle.

    Inside this room, you will perform the O Mother (Gesture) while facing the altar to reveal the steps leading to Hinterlands. Inside this new area, you will be able to progress Count Ymir and Jolan's questlines. Head inside and travel to Finger Ruins of Dheo and sound the bell. When you're done here, return once again to Count Ymir to finish up his questline. You may go to this page to see the full details.

    Jagged Peak

    • Speak with Igon and accept Igon's Furled Finger

After the fight, you will find Igon sitting nearby and praises you for your bravery and for defeating the dragons. He will hand Igon's Furled Finger and will ask you to summon him when battling Bayle the Dread.

    Locate the Spiritspring behind him and use it to climb the area above you. Make your way to the arena of the Ancient Dragon Senessax, defeat it, and head up the mountain again. Keep going up here until you eventually reach Bayle the Dread's arena.

    While this boss is completely optional, we are including it in the game progression as it plays a role in progressing Igon and Dragon Communion Priestess' questlines. Head inside the arena, and look for Igon's summon sign. Beckon him into the fight and defeat Bayle the Dread. After the fight, you will be able to loot Igon's items (if you summoned him) and then return to the Grand Altar of Dragon Communion where her quest will be concluded.

    Shadow Keep (Main Gate)

    • Enter Shadow Keep through Main Gate
    • Defeat Golden Hippopotamus
    • Do the Hornsent vs Leda fight (We recommend on taking Hornsent's side to defeat Leda together; use the golden summon sign)
    • Make your way to West Rampart - Site of Grace
    • Speak with Sir Ansbach
    • Obtain Secret Rite Scroll
    • Go to Storehouse, Seventh Floor and speak with Redmane Freyja exhaust her dialogues
    • Return to Sir Ansbach, tell her about Freyja, exhaust his dialogues
    • Give the Secret Rite Scroll to Sir Ansbach and accept Letter for Freyja
    • Take the letter to Freyja and get the Golden Lion Shield from her

    Go through the Main Gate and ride the elevator going up. Enter the plaza and defeat the Golden Hippopotamus here. Your exploration will begin by locating the shortcut access around the area, and back to the Main Plaza. After that, you will take part in the Hornsent and Leda fight. Then, look for the West Rampart - Site of Grace, which you will use later.

Not summoning him in Messmer fight will prompt him to take revenge against you and will show up as an ally to Needle Knight Leda later.

    Enir-Ilim

    • Defeat the final boss

Continue to go to the top of the dungeon where you will meet a familiar boss, the Promised Consort Radahn and Miquella will also appear here.

    If you progressed Thiollier's questline, he can be summoned to fight the final boss, and then you can pick up his items within the arena after the fight. Then, travel to the Garden of Deep Purple and take the St. Trina's Blossom from her last position.
